Biography

Dr. Nguyen Phuoc Quy Duy is currently an Assistant Professor at the College of Engineering and Computer Science, VinUniversity, Vietnam. He earned his Ph.D. from Monash University, Australia in 2018 and subsequently worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Singapore – MIT Alliance for Research and Technology (SMART).

His research interests include sustainable mobility, traffic safety, travel behaviour, transport modelling, and transport economics. He has published over 60 papers in Q1 journals with an h-index of 32. He currently leads the Sustainable Travel and Economic Behaviour Research Group (STEB-RG) at the University of Danang and serves on the editorial boards of leading journals such as Travel Behaviour and Society (SSCI, Q1).
